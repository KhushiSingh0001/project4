Local Tips

Food

Tokyo is famous for sushi, ramen, and tempura. Be sure to visit Tsukiji Fish Market for fresh seafood.

Transportation

Use the efficient Tokyo Metro system to get around the city. Purchase a JR Pass for tourists for easy access.

Culture

Respect for tradition is essential. Always remove your shoes when entering temples and homes.
